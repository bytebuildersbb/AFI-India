Member Eligibility and Rights:-

Any adult person having registration as doctor under the AYUSH system established by the Department of Health and Family Welfare Department, Government of India, or any of its department, constitutional Corporations or Central Medical Councils or the recognized State Medical Councils or Boards and he is accepting the Objects and programs of AFI shall be entitled to become a Member of AFI by applying on a prescribed form, but such persons practicing Yoga and Naturopathy system who have been/having degree/diploma in the streams of the Institutions detailed in 2001 Edition of Indian System of Medicines & Homeopathy published by the Health and Family Welfare Ministry, Government of India. But the doctors of Yoga and Naturopathy shall be accepted as members only when they submit an Affidavit in a prescribed form with their Membership form to the effect that they are working only in Yoga and naturopathy and shall continue with the same only.

End of Membership:

The membership of the members can be ended on finding any of the reasons mentioned below:

  1. On the death of a member,
  2. On becoming mentally unfit or insolvent,
  3. On the non-payment of membership fee,
  4. On tendering resignation to the Central Council,
  5. On acting against the interest of the Organization and Constitution, and disciplinary grounds.
  6. On being punished by the court of any law for any act of moral turpitude, after serving the member a notice of one month and on explanation of the member and on hearing him the Central Council/Committee shall expel him from the membership.
  7. In such an event the Managing Committee of AFI, after conducting inquiry can pass resolution to expel a member for a limited period or forever. The person so expelled shall be losing all his rights of membership.
  8. Any member who will file suit before the Court against the Federation, its Committee/sub-committee and their office bearers without presenting his case before the Central Council of AFI and before imparting the decision by the Central Council, the membership of such person shall be immediately automatically expelled.

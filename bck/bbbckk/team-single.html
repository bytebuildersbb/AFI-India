Dr. Abhishek Gupta

Founder

Dr. Abhishek Gupta has over 13 years of diverse experience in the Ayurveda field. Having exceptional communication skills to lead and interface with all levels of leadership, and also has a clear understanding of Ayurveda concepts and problems related to its graduates and for the last 12 years he has led Ayurveda related issues and its businesses in different parts of the country.

Dr. Abhishek has done Bachelor of Ayurveda Medicine and Surgery (B.A.M.S.) from Rajiv Gandhi University - Bangalore, Karnataka (India).

“Dr. Abhishek has been with NirogStreet (India's largest digital platform of Ayurveda) as a Co-founder & CMO for the last 4 years.”

Prior to his role at NirogStreet, Dr. Abhishek was the Director of the Brahm Ayurved. He also worked with Apollo Pharmacy as an "Advisor-Ayurveda" in the product development division and also was the National General Secretary of AYUSH Association - India.  

Dr. Abhishek is passionate about writing & editing in Ayurvedic Books, Magazines & articles. Over 500+ Ayurveda issues centric, disease-centric articles/blogs, case records and many other topics-related articles have been published in various National Newspapers, Health magazines, websites etc.

Co-Authored in books on “Clinical Practice in Ayurveda” & “Sampurna Swasthya” presently in progress.

Dr. Abhishek is a firm believer in the power of common people, his mantra of success is that a handful of simple, determined people can change the course of history. He also has a simple dream behind establishing the Ayurveda Federation of India, from which he sees with open eyes that the people of Ayurveda should be self-reliant, capable, confident and can make an impressive contribution to public health through Ayurveda. To fulfill this dream, by getting a permanent and right solution to all the problems and legal hurdles related to Ayurveda, every district of the country should have at least 10 multispecialty hospitals of Ayurveda and 100 such highly equipped Ayurveda Day Care Centers in the next 10 years where the general public able to get high-quality treatment of Ayurveda!
